Install the library:

$ mvn install:install-file -Dfile=./AlexLib.jar -DgroupId=alex -DartifactId=AlexLib -Dversion=1.0 -Dpackaging=jar


Install the dependency in pom.xml.

<dependency>
    <groupId>alex</groupId>
    <artifactId>AlexLib</artifactId>
    <version>1.0</version>
</dependency>
